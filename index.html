HELPING HAND

"Helping Hand" is a groundbreaking online platform dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare accessibility. We have meticulously compiled a comprehensive database of essential medical services, including hospitals, labs, blood banks, ambulance services, and pharmacies. Our platform serves as a one-stop destination for individuals seeking critical healthcare information.

Key Features:

A comprehensive healthcare database for hospitals, labs, blood banks, ambulance services, and pharmacies.
Detailed facility profiles for informed decision-making.
An intuitive, user-friendly interface.
Real-time emergency ambulance information.
Community reviews for transparency and informed choices.

MEDICAL STORES

A medical store, also known as a pharmacy or drugstore, offers
prescription and non-prescription medications, medical supplies,
and healthcare products to the public.

LABORATORY

Medical labs are facilities where diagnostic tests and analyses
are conducted on patient samples to assist in medical diagnoses
and monitoring of health conditions.

BLOOD BANK

A blood bank is a facility that collects, stores, and distributes
blood and blood products for medical purposes, such as
transfusions and treatments.
